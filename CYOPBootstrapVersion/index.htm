About Me

My name is Michael Althoff and welcome to my website! I am currently a Computer Science major at University of North Carolina at Asheville. This site started as a project for a computer science class I was taking.

As you may have noticed one of my interests is motorcycling. This is mainly a place for sharing information I have acquired on track days and motorcycling for those new to the sport.

Have a look around

Take a look at the Track Day Information page for a general overview of what track days are and what you need to do to get started.

The Track Day Resource page includes a list of some track day providers and several links to online retailers for motorcycle gear that I have used.

The YouTube area has a some additional information for a track day beginner as well as a link to my YouTube channel.
